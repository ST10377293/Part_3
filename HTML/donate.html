Donate to Harvest Hope

Your donation can make a difference in the lives of those struggling with hunger in our community. Your support allows us to provide nutritious meals to families, children, and seniors in need.

Other Ways to Donate

If you prefer to make a donation in person, you can visit one of our food bank locations. You can also donate by mail by sending a check to:

Harvest Hope Food Bank
2220 Shop Rd
Columbia, SC 29201

For information on donating food or hosting a food drive, please visit our Contact Us page.

Donation Options

There are several ways to support our organization:

  • One-time Donation: Make a one-time donation online or by mail.
  • Recurring Donation: Set up a monthly recurring donation to provide ongoing support.
Donate Now

Impact

Your donation can make a real difference in the lives of those we serve. With your support, we can provide nutritious meals to families in need, and help them achieve long-term food security.

Here are some examples of how your donation can help:

  • $25 can provide a family with a week's worth of groceries
  • $50 can provide a month's worth of groceries for a senior citizen
  • $100 can provide backpacks filled with school supplies for 10 children

Corporate Giving

Join other businesses in supporting our mission to fight hunger in the community. Corporate giving is a great way to give back and make a positive impact on those in need.

Here are some ways your company can support our work:

  • Corporate Sponsorship: Sponsor one of our events or programs to increase visibility and show your support.
  • Employee Giving: Encourage your employees to give back by setting up a workplace giving campaign.
  • In-Kind Donations: Donate products or services that can help us operate more efficiently and serve more people.
