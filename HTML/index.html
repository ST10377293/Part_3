Overview

We are a community food bank dedicated to fighting hunger and food insecurity in the Farmers' Markets area. Our mission is to provide nutritious food to those in need, while also addressing the root causes of hunger and working towards long-term solutions.

Mission statement

Our mission at the Harvest Hope is to provide food and resources to individuals and families in need in our community. We believe that everyone deserves access to healthy and nutritious food, and we are committed to fighting hunger and promoting food security in our community.

Main activties

Food collection

The community food bank might collect food donations from local businesses, organizations, and individuals. They might also purchase food in bulk to distribute to those in need.

Food distribution

The community food bank would distribute food to individuals and families in need through a food pantry or similar program.

Volunteer recruitment and management

The community food bank would recruit and manage volunteers to help with various tasks, such as sorting and distributing food donations, fundraising, and community outreach.

Fundraising

The community food bank would raise funds to support their programs and operations, through events, grants, and donations.

Community outreach and education

The community food bank would engage in community outreach and education to raise awareness about hunger and food insecurity in the community. This might include partnering with schools and other organizations to provide educational programming on healthy eating and nutrition.
